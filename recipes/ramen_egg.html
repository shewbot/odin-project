Ramen Eggs

Ingredients:

  • Defatted Pork Braising Liquid

OR

  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sake
  • Kombu
  • Honey or Sugar
  • Scallions and Ginger

Steps:

  1. Boil eggs for 6:30 minutes and drop into ice bath.
  2. Crack and peel eggs.
  3. Drop into a container or cover with pork braising liquid or other ingredients.
  4. Marinate for 1-7 days.
